Distance Learning Resources

  1. https://www.khanacademy.org/computing
    Khan Academy is a non-profit organization that offers great resources on many subjects from k-12 and beyond.
  2. https://www.exploratorium.edu/snacks The exploatorium is based in San Francisco, CA, and it is a museum of artts, science, and technology. They
    offer mini science experiments that can be incorporated with distance learning.
  3. https://edpuzzle.com/ This is a great interactive website where students are able to watch videos and answer questions as the video
    progresses. Teachers are able to monitor the percentage of video that is watched and create questions for any
    video on YouTube.
